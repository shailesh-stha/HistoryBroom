1. Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle icon in your browser toolbar and pin History Broom so you can access it easily.

✍️
2. Add Keywords

Open the extension popup and add keywords or domain names you want to automatically scrub from your history.

⚡
3. Set Automation

Choose when cleanup happens: on browser start, when closing a tab, on an idle timer, or instantly in real-time.

🛡️
4. Add Exceptions

Need to keep some sites? Add exceptions in the popup to ensure specific URLs are never deleted.

Zero Data Collection Pledge

History Broom operates 100% locally on your device. We never collect, monitor, or transmit your browsing data, analytics, or settings to any external servers. The permissions requested are used strictly to find and erase the keywords you specify, directly within your browser.
